Krazy Kat Simulator

Krazy Kat Simulator is my first game, and I am making it with Ryan Grube. It's general premise is the same as any clicker game, just with cats. You pet a cat to get money, and you buy upgrades to pet the cat faster or automatically, and you try to get the most money. It is coded in javasctript and HTML, and I made the base clicker in one day, and now we are continuing development over the forseeable future.
